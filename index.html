ABOUT PORTFOLIO PUBLICATIONS CONNECT MERCH
CRAIG FOUTS

I am a graduate student in applied math at Columbia University working as a member of the Computational Morphogenomics Group to develop probabilistic models of self-organizing systems and morphogenesis. I also work as a visiting researcher at the New York Genome Center, where I help develop machine learning tools for genomic research as a member of the Technology Innovation Laboratory. Outside of research, I enjoy skiing, scuba diving, inline/ice skating, and playing the violin/lyre.
