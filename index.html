CRAIG FOUTS

I am a current graduate student in applied mathematics at Columbia University and a visiting scientist at the New York Genome Center, where I help develop computational tools for genomic research as part of the Technology Innovation Lab. At Columbia, I study statistical models of self-organizing systems and morphogenesis as a member of the Computational Morphogenomics Group. Outside of research, I enjoy skiing, inline skating, and playing the violin.
